## Announcements

We’re excited to invite you to this month’s [Socratic Seminar](/en/about) at BitDevs Cotonou!

Whether you're a full-time developer, part-time builder, entrepreneur, or simply curious about the technical side of Bitcoin, this meetup is for you.

This month’s session will be packed with learning and interaction. We’ll begin with an introduction to BitDevs Cotonou and our sponsor Btrust for new members. Then, we’ll host a presentation from Bitshala, a Bitcoin development school based in India, where you’ll learn about their training approach and growth opportunities.

We’ll also run a hands-on workshop focused on integrating Bitcoin payments for businesses using BTCPay Server and the Blink API.

To wrap up, join us for a networking cocktail session to connect with fellow Bitcoiners in a relaxed environment.

This seminar is a great opportunity to ask questions, contribute to thoughtful discussion, and strengthen ties within the local Bitcoin community.

A big thank you to our sponsor [Btrust](https://btrust.tech) for supporting the growth of a free and decentralized Bitcoin ecosystem in Benin.
